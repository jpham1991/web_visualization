Wind Speed

Wind speed appears to peak at +48 and +73 degrees north of the equator. At +48 degrees north, wind speed is about 36 mph, and at +73 degrees north, wind speed is about 34 mph. The lowest wind speeds of below 5 mph appears to occur between -55 degrees south of the equator and +68 degrees north of the equator. There is not much of a notable pattern between wind speed and distance from equator. A larger sample size might helps us determine if there really is a positive or negative correlation between city proximity to the equator and wind speed.
